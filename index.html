About US

Mike Dixon

TRUE BMX Pro Shop is owned by Mike Dixon, a native of South Carolina. He grew up in Blythewood, SC, and began racing BMX at the age of 15. His passion for BMX began to grow as he continued racing throughout high school. In 2009, he raced the NBL Grand Nationals in Louisville, KY, and was 8th national that year. He retired from the Army in 2011 after 28 years of service, but never retired from his love of BMX. His dream was to open a bike shop that specialized in BMX and after hearing of the plans to build a supercross track in Rock Hill, he knew that was where he wanted to be. The location at Cherry Road became available, a mere 1.5 miles from the Novant Health Supercross Track, so in April 2016, he opened the doors of TRUE BMX Pro Shop.
